Frequently Asked Questions

How do squads work?

Squads are your core friend groups. Create one with your closest friends or join existing squads. Each squad has its own chat and can create activities that all members can join and earn points from.

Can I join activities from squads I'm not in?

Absolutely! You'll see all nearby activities on the map. While non-members see limited details, you can request to join any activity. If accepted, you'll be added to that activity's chat and can check in to participate.

How does the points system work?

When you check in at an activity, your squad earns points. Each squad member can check in once per activity. Squads with 3+ members appear on the leaderboard, creating friendly competition to see who hangs out the most.

What happens to activity chats?

Activity chats are created when an activity is made and expire 6 hours after the activity's start time. After expiration, you can still view messages but can't send new ones‚Äîperfect for preserving memories without endless notifications.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes! Your location data, squad chats, and activity details are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties. Only squad members see full activity details.
