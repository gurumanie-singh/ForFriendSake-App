what it does

from group chats to
real hangouts.

ForFriendSake transforms your friend groups into active squads. Plan activities, discover what's happening nearby, and compete on leaderboards — all in one place.

Squad Creation
Form squads with your closest friends or join existing ones. Each squad gets its own chat for planning and staying connected.
Activity Planning
Create activities in your squad chat and invite members to join — from casual hangouts to epic weekend adventures.
Map Discovery
See all activities happening around you on an interactive map. Request to join or open yours to the public to expand your circle.
Location Check-ins
Show up, check in with GPS verification, and earn squad points. Proof that your squad doesn't just talk — you actually do it.
Leaderboards
Squads with 3+ members compete on global leaderboards. Claim the top spot and prove you're the most active friend group around.
Activity Chats
Each activity gets its own temporary chat that expires 6 hours after the event starts — coordinate in real-time, preserve the memory after.

privacy

your data stays
protected.

Built on Firebase with strict security rules. Location data is only collected when you need it, and your squad activity stays between your squad.

Location on demand
Location data is only collected during active check-ins. We never track you in the background.
Encrypted data
End-to-end encryption for sensitive data. Firebase Security Rules protect every collection.
Squad-private chats
Squad messages and activity chats are visible only to members. Non-members see limited public details.
GDPR & CCPA compliant
We follow global privacy standards. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.
