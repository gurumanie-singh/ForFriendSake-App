What is Vocabrawl?

Vocabrawl takes the five-letter guessing game you already love and makes it social. Every match is a quick, satisfying brain workout you can finish in a couple of minutes — alone whenever you want, or head-to-head with friends through Game Center.

Two ways to play

Solo

Jump straight in — no sign-in required. Take on simulated opponents at Easy, Medium, or Hard difficulty and sharpen your guessing before you challenge real people.

Online

Sign in to Game Center and battle friends in two flavors: Competitive, where everyone races to solve the same word in real time, and Turn-Based, where you trade guesses and rack up points.

How to play

  1. Guess a five-letter word.
  2. Green tiles are right and in place; yellow tiles are in the word but misplaced; gray tiles aren’t in the word.
  3. Use the clues to crack the answer in six tries.
  4. Win by solving first (Competitive) or scoring the most points (Turn-Based).
