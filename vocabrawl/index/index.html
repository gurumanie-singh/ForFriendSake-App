what it does

five letters.
infinite rivalry.

Vocabrawl takes the five-letter guessing game you already love and makes it social. Every match is a quick brain workout — compete in real time or take your turn whenever.

Real-Time Competitive
All players race to solve the same hidden word simultaneously. See your rivals' colored tile progress without seeing their guesses.
Turn-Based Duels
Take turns, one guess at a time. Score points for every correct tile and earn a bonus if you crack the word. Highest score wins.
Solo Mode
Jump straight in with no sign-in required. Sharpen your word skills against simulated opponents any time, anywhere.
Game Center Integration
Invite friends, get matched with opponents, and manage turn-based matches — all through Apple's native Game Center.
No Account Required
Play solo without any sign-in. Go online any time by connecting your existing Apple ID — no separate account to create.
Light & Dark Mode
Plays beautifully in both light and dark mode, adapting to your system preference or your choice in Settings.

how to play

three rules.
endless strategy.

01
Guess a five-letter word
Type any valid five-letter English word and submit your guess. You have up to six attempts to find the answer.
02
Read the color clues
Green means right letter, right place. Yellow means right letter, wrong place. Gray means the letter isn't in the word.
03
Outsmart your opponents
Use the clues strategically. In Competitive mode, finish in fewer guesses. In Turn-Based, maximize your tile score each round.
04
Climb the leaderboard
Ranked by fewest guesses, then fastest time. Win enough and you'll be the undisputed word champion of your friend group.

privacy

your data stays
yours.

Vocabrawl is private by design. No accounts, no email addresses, no tracking. Online play is handled entirely by Apple's Game Center.

No data collected
Vocabrawl does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No name, no email, no analytics.
Apple handles identity
Online play uses Apple Game Center. Your identity is managed by Apple under their privacy policy — not ours.
On-device only
Solo games run entirely on your device. No custom servers. No backend. No cloud storage of any kind.
GDPR & COPPA compliant
Because we collect no personal data, compliance is straightforward. We do not track. We do not sell. Ever.
