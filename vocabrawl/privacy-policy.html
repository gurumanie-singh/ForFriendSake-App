Plain language first. Vocabrawl is a word game. This policy explains exactly what data the app does and does not collect — which is very little, by design. We do not sell your data. We never will.
01

The Short Version

Vocabrawl does not collect, store, sell, or transmit your personal data. There are no accounts to create, no email addresses to enter, no analytics SDKs, and no advertising. Online multiplayer is handled entirely by Apple's Game Center — your identity stays with Apple, not with us.

Solo games run entirely on your device. Turn-based and competitive online matches use Apple's Game Center servers. We have no backend of our own.

02

Information We Collect

Vocabrawl collects the following information to provide its functionality.

data why we use it
Appearance preference Saved locally on your device so the app remembers your chosen light or dark mode setting
Onboarding state A simple flag saved locally to track whether you have completed the intro — never leaves your device

That is the complete list. No names, no email addresses, no device identifiers, no analytics events, no crash reports sent to our servers. Everything above is stored using your device's standard local preferences (UserDefaults) and is removed when you delete the app.

03

What We Don't Collect

For absolute clarity:

No name or username No email address No phone number No location data No analytics or tracking No advertising identifiers No payment information No crash reports to our servers

Vocabrawl is free and contains no advertising. It has no custom backend server. We have no database of users and never will.
04

Game Center

Online competitive and turn-based matches use Apple Game Center. When you play online, Apple manages your player identity, matchmaking, and match state on its own servers under Apple's Privacy Policy.

Vocabrawl never receives your Apple ID, your real name, or your email address. We receive only the anonymized player information Game Center provides so a match can take place — specifically, an anonymized Game Center player identifier and your Game Center display name. We do not log, store, or retain this information after the match session ends.

Game Center accounts for minors are governed by Apple's Family Sharing and parental controls. Apple handles all age restrictions and parental consent requirements for Game Center use.

05

Data Stored on Your Device

Vocabrawl saves a small amount of non-sensitive information locally on your device using the system's standard preferences storage (UserDefaults):

Appearance mode preference Intro completion flag

This data never leaves your device, is not backed up to any server, and is permanently removed when you delete the app from your device.

06

Third-Party Services

Vocabrawl uses only one third-party service:

service purpose
Apple Game Center Player identity, matchmaking, and turn-based match state for online play. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

No analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, no crash reporting services, no social media pixels. The app uses Apple's system frameworks only.

07

Children's Privacy

Because Vocabrawl collects no personal data from any user, it meets the baseline requirements of children's privacy regulations including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) by virtue of not processing personal information.

Game Center accounts for users under 13 are governed by Apple's own parental consent and Family Sharing controls. We have no ability to verify or influence user age — Apple manages this entirely.

08

GDPR & CCPA

Vocabrawl does not process personal data as defined under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Because no personal data is collected, stored, or processed by us:

No consent required for data processing No data to access, correct, or delete No data sold — ever No data transferred internationally by us

If you have privacy concerns related to your Apple ID or Game Center data, those are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy and must be directed to Apple.
09

Data Security

Because Vocabrawl stores no personal data on any server we control, there is no database to breach. Local preferences stored on your device are protected by iOS's built-in security model, including device encryption.

Online match data transmitted through Game Center is secured by Apple's infrastructure under their security standards.

10

Changes to This Policy

Continued use of Vocabrawl after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms. If you do not agree with any changes, you should stop using the app and contact us using the details below.
